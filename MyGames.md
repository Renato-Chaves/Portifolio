Restoration Frontier (2024)
Engine: Unity 3D | Multiplayer
A cooperative multiplayer game where a group of robots cleans pollution from a planet. Players collect scrap, bring it to recycling stations to build automation machines, destroy pollution sources, and ultimately reach and destroy a mega-factory. Features pollution clouds that block vision, resource farming, and map progression.
Links: YouTube gameplay showcase | GitHub
(Not yet published on Itch/Gamejolt)
HTML5 demo: No

Rushing to the Top (2021)
Engine: Unity 2D | GameJaaj 5 (theme: "Torres")
A roguelite 2D shooter. The ocean is rising — survive by reaching the top of a building. Buy and upgrade guns before each run, shoot enemies floor by floor, fix elevators to keep climbing. The water level rises over time — take too long on a floor and you die.
Link: Gamejolt
HTML5 demo: No

Plus and Minus (2021)
Engine: Unity 3D | Game Jam
A two-player physics game. Starts as a 1v1 fight in a ring — but there are hidden clues on how to escape your chains. Find them and the game shifts into a cooperative 2-player escape platformer where both players are physically attached and must navigate together using physics.
Link: Itch.io
HTML5 demo: No
